Who Can Be a Food Donor?

Any business that has excess food that could be going to waste can connect with their local Food Rescue US location and discuss donating their food.

What food can be donated:

  • Fresh produce
  • Dairy products
  • Fresh or frozen meats
  • Baked goods
  • Pre-packaged foods
  • Dry goods, non-perishables
